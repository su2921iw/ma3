Enjoy viewing our wide variety of Venetian masks imported from and beautifully handmade in the traditional spirit of Venice, Italy.

Mask Italia is proud to feature Venetian mask designs from over fifty artisans from Venice, Verona, and Florence. We travel to Venice six times every year to bring the latest venetian mask designs and techniques to the American market. All Venetian masks are handmade and each one is individually selected for our collection for its quality and beauty. The masks can be used for home décor, wall hangings, sculpture, and of course for wearing.

Mask Italia, located in San Francisco, can ship masks anywhere in the world.
